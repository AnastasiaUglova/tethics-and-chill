What is Agentic Tech?

Products and protocols that promote human dignity by protecting individual control over privacy, data, assets, and information.

Agentic Tech is an emerging category — one that is just now defining itself. It spans cognitive security, data infrastructure, AI/ML, homomorphic encryption, decentralized tech, zero-knowledge cryptography, and other privacy-enhancing technologies.

See ex/ante’s market map of the Agentic Tech landscape → Read the research thesis that coined the category →

Data Ownership & Privacy

Advancing user privacy and individual control over personal data through encryption, FHE, MPC, and other PETs.

Security & Protection

Protecting individuals and organizations from virtual and physical threats — cybersecurity, risk mitigation, OSINT, and defensive solutions.

Information Integrity

Promoting transparency and quality of public information — media forensics, algorithmic transparency, anti-censorship, and countering disinformation.

Community Norms

Candor and kindness.

Because we host conversations that push boundaries and interrogate dominant paradigms, we curate spaces that feel respectful and inspire curiosity. We have a few community norms to maintain the trust and integrity of the group.

Discretion

Pressure-test ideas without the panopticon of public opinion. We never record sessions and ask permission before sharing outside the group.

Maturity

Be kind. Embrace a plurality of perspectives, create space for others, disagree respectfully, and change your mind when presented with better information.

Curiosity

Come with genuine curiosity about the intersection of technology and human agency. Seek to understand rather than to prove someone wrong.

Signal

Share opportunities, job leads, and deal flow. Ask for help. But don't shill your warez without context, and keep the memes well-timed.
